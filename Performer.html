Stephanie Jones: Performer

Stephanie Jones finds constant joy performing improvisational and sketch comedy. A Boston native, she previously frequented the stages of ImprovBoston and ImprovAsylum. During her eight years in Chicago, she performed at iO theater, Playground Theater, as well as with the cast of Improvised Jane Austen. In spite of her difficulty memorizing lines, she has also participated in a few scripted performances, both on and off camera (Couchsurferz, Bikini Werewolf Beach, and a couple of other projects of varying quality).
